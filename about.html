About Us

Events_Planning is a full service experiential event management company based in Kolkata. We have a pan India project footprint and also have a presence in Mumbai.

Our approach is highly client-oriented and we take pride in our record excellence and quality of our services. Our portfolio includes a wide range of services and solutions in the areas of corporate events, weddings, personalised celebrations, special events and concerts.

It is our endeavour to understand your event requirements, your desires and your objectives and accordingly design and execute an event that will help you achieve the desired results. This way we are able to offer you customised solutions and meet your expectations.

Our mission is to bring your vision to life by creating one-of-a-kind events that you and your guests will remember for a lifetime.

Our sole objective is to provide customised solutions and 24x7 excellent support services on all event management and activation needs of our clients. Efficiency of resources starts with sound strategic thinking and direction. With a dedicated and experienced team of event professionals, we understand, deliver and provide on-time and customised event solutions to our clients.

We are what our clients say about us: Professional - Articulate - Flexible – Approachable.
